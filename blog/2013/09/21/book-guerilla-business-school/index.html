Guerilla Business School

by Wojciech Adam Koszek, Sep 21, 2013, Menlo Park, CA

Don't remember much from it.

Live recordings from countries other than Poland let me realize how different the society is. It’s very hard for me to believe such a class would have taken place in Poland.

Some parts were nice, but some were a scam. The thing where Eker encouraged people to go and lie in the stores to get free stuff was kind of low in my opinion. It seemed almost like stealing form the store owners by stretching their trust to you.

Valuable study as long as you’ll judge all subjects presented.
